Our History

TechCon began as a small gathering of software developers and technology enthusiasts over a decade ago. What started as a single-day meetup has grown into a multi-day international conference that brings together leaders from across the tech industry.

Over the years, TechCon has expanded its focus from traditional software engineering topics to include emerging fields such as artificial intelligence, cloud-native architectures, cybersecurity, and data science. Each edition of TechCon builds on the last, fostering a community of continuous learning and innovation.

From its humble beginnings to its current global reach, TechCon remains committed to providing a platform where ideas are shared, connections are made, and the future of technology is shaped.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon 2024 is to empower developers, innovators, and decision-makers with the knowledge, tools, and inspiration they need to build the next generation of technology solutions responsibly and sustainably.

We aim to create an inclusive environment where experienced professionals and newcomers alike can learn from each other, collaborate on real-world challenges, and explore how technology can positively impact communities around the world.

Through keynote talks, hands-on workshops, panel discussions, and networking sessions, TechCon 2024 encourages attendees to think critically, share openly, and build with purpose.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Amina Okafor

Dr. Amina Okafor is a leading researcher in artificial intelligence ethics. At TechCon 2022, she delivered a keynote on building fair and transparent machine learning systems, inspiring organizations to rethink how they design and deploy AI at scale.

Lucas Martinez

Lucas Martinez, a pioneer in cloud-native infrastructure, has helped companies transition from monolithic architectures to microservices-based systems. At TechCon 2021, he shared practical strategies for improving reliability and scalability using modern DevOps practices.

Sophia Chen

Sophia Chen is a cybersecurity expert specializing in securing large-scale distributed systems. Her sessions at TechCon have focused on emerging threats, secure-by-design principles, and how teams can collaborate to protect critical infrastructure.

Each year, TechCon welcomes a diverse lineup of speakers who are shaping the future of technology in academia, industry, and the open-source community.
